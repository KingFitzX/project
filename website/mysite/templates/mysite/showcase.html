Meaning of inputs

Country A is the country you are acting as, and Country B is the opposing country.

"Cares About" - Represents how much the nation cares about the contention in question (e.g. human resources in country, oil imports).
"No. Nukes" - Represents how many nuclear weapons the nation has.
"Aggression" - Represents how willing a country is to attack with nuclear weapons or go to war.
"International Rep" - Represents how much international support a country has.
"Population" - Represents the population for the nation.
"Provocation" - Represents whether the country has been bombed in the scenario.
